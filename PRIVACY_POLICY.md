# Privacy Policy for PurefusionIPTV

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

PurefusionIPTV ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our Android application.

## 1. Important Disclaimer
PurefusionIPTV is a **Media Player** application. 
* We **do not** provide, host, or distribute any media content or streams. 
* Users are solely responsible for providing their own content (e.g., M3U playlists, EPG URLs, or login credentials).
* We have no affiliation with any third-party content providers.

## 2. Information We Collect
We value your privacy and aim for minimal data collection:
* **User-Provided Content:** Any URLs, usernames, or passwords entered into the app are stored locally on your device and are used only to connect to your specified services. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
* **Device Information:** We may collect non-identifying information such as device model, OS version, and crash logs (via Google Play Console) to improve app stability and performance.
* **Identifiers:** We do not collect personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

## 3. How We Use Information
The information processed by the app is used strictly for:
* Providing the media playback functionality.
* Debugging and fixing technical issues.
* Ensuring compatibility with your hardware.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Since users provide their own stream sources, you may be subject to the privacy policies of your IPTV service provider. We are not responsible for the data collection practices of these third-party services.

## 5. Security
We implement standard security measures to protect the data stored locally on your device. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
